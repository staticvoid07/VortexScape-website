Welcome to VortexScape!


A modified version of 2004scape that functions as an idle game.

Everyone is an ironman, every item is stackable, most skilling actions will infinitely repeat, skilling resources never deplete, you don't lose items on death, and Hans will complete quests for you for 100k each (you still get the quest reward), heroes and legends quest cost 500k.

Every skill has an idle way of being trained, agility courses will auto-complete, fishing spots never move, pickpocketing and stealing from stalls repeat, and much more.


To play open the client and click on "Existing user", type a user and password and if it doesn't already exist you will be registered in.


DO NOT FORGET YOUR PASSWORD, I HAVE NO EASY WAY OF RESETTING THEM YET.


This project is open source.
